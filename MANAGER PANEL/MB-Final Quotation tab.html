Billing Settings Management

Commercial Billing

  • All commercial recurring services (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly) are billed once at the end of the month.
  • Commercial invoices are consolidated into one monthly invoice, regardless of service frequency.
  • Applies to all commercial recurring cleanings.

Residential Billing Recurring

  • Recurring residential services receive one invoice on the 15th of the month.
  • Services include weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly cleaning schedules.

Residential Billing One-Time

  • One-time residential cleanings are billed once upon service completion.
  • Invoice is generated immediately after service confirmation.

One-Time Cleans (General)

  • All one-time cleans are billed immediately after the cleaning is completed.
  • Includes both residential and commercial one-time services.

Invoicing Automation

End-of-day automation reviews logged work hours.

Invoices are built automatically based on:

  • Hours worked
  • Recurrence rules
  • Service type

Financing Automation
