Step 8 — Manager: Assign Cleaner(s)

Workflow Context: This panel is displayed inside the Manage Booking module. Only appointments with status "Paid" or "Ready for Scheduling" trigger this interface. Managers can assign a cleaner or team for each appointment. Once assigned, the system updates the appointment details and sends notifications to the assigned cleaner(s) and the client.
